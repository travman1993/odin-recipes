Jalapeno Poppers

These baked jalapeño poppers are the ultimate game day snack! They're crispy on top, creamy in the middle, and packed with delicious savory flavor. If anything could turn me into a sports fan, it’d be these baked jalapeño poppers! Like all the best game day snacks theyre simple, tasty, and really darn fun.

Ingredients

  • cream cheese
  • shredded cheddar
  • jalapenos
  • bacon

Directions

  1. Make the filling by micing both cream cheese and shredded cheddar in a bowl, set it to the side
  2. Cut jalapeño down the middle, remove seeds and vains
  3. Fill each jalapeno half with filling made previously
  4. Wrap each half with one strip of bacon
  5. In an oven preheated to 400 degrees F, your jalapeño poppers should be perfectly cooked with crispy bacon in 25-35 minutes.
